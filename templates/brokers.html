Broker Ranking

Full broker ranking table across the cleaned lending dataset.

Score logic: Risk Score is produced by a logistic regression model trained on loan priority, rate, LVR, loan term, and principal size. Each loan receives a predicted overdue probability, then broker scores are calculated as the average predicted probability scaled to 0-100. Overdue % is the realized share of loans past repayment and not yet closed. Risk Level is assigned as High Risk (>=75), Elevated Risk (>=50), Moderate Risk (>=25), or Low Risk.
